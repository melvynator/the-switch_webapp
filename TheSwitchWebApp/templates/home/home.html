The Switch

Allowing you to safely travel, knowing that in case of trouble your relatives will be warned.

The essential application for travelers and expatriates
to never let you relatives without news.

We all had this feeling one day when doing a high risk activity that our family will never be warned,
or know that something went wrong. But with the Switch, no more!

Security

Because this is very personal data, no statistics, no analyses are done. All data from your email used to register to the text for your relatives are encrypted with the best cryptology algorithm.

Social media

We are living in the 21 century, this is why we choose to add a feature to allow the transmission of your social media account to your relative.

File management

Some file need to be transmited. So we add a feature allowing you to transmit file as well like: pictures, text file, PDF, etc ...

Customisation

The content you want to transmit is customizable. You choose who and what people will received. You can as well personalize the duration after which you want to start to transmit the informations.
